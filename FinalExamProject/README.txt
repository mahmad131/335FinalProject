App Description - One or two line description.
Using the PokeAPI, users can search for their favorite Pokémon, and see a picture of the Pokémon, its base stats, the moves it can learn, along with its types and other information.
Additionally, within a session, users can access their search history to get a quick snapshot of the pokemon they've recently looked up in our Pokédex.
